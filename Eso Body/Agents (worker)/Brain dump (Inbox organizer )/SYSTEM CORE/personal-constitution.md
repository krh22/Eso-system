## My Philosophy of Balance

All areas of life are important. None is more important than the others.
What I protect is the balance between them.

- **Body** — trained, fed, rested, maintained
- **Mind** — learning, building, thinking clearly
- **Spirit** — connected, aware, at peace
- **Relationships** — family protected, friends chosen carefully, community growing
- **Finances** — enough for freedom, growing toward abundance
- **Work** — electrical engineering completed, systems built, vision pursued

When one area collapses, the others feel it.
My system exists to monitor all areas and alert me before collapse happens.

---

## How I Make Decisions

1. Does this align with my truth?
2. Does this protect my peace?
3. Does this harm anyone?
4. Does this move me toward balance or away from it?
5. Would the best version of me — aware, integrated, free — make this choice?

If the answer to 1, 2, 4, and 5 is yes and the answer to 3 is no — I proceed.
