# Saving Goals — ECS

This file defines the strategic targets for the saving module.

## Saving Tiers

1. **Tier 1: Emergency Buffer**
   - **Target:** KES 36,000 (3 months of Layer 1 expenses).
   - **Status:** Primary goal. This must be filled before Tier 2 begins.
   
2. **Tier 2: Life Upgrade Fund**
   - **Target:** KES 100,000.
   - **Purpose:** Equipment, business startup costs, or major purchases.
   
3. **Tier 3: Wealth Building**
   - **Target:** Indefinite.
   - **Purpose:** Long-term savings and capital growth above Tier 2.

## Rules

- **Activation:** The saving module only activates after the spending module is confirmed stable.
- **Funding:** Money flows from the Layer 2 "Buffer and Rollover" bucket into saving automatically.
- **Sealed Buckets:** Saving buckets are sealed containers, identical to the spending module protocol.
- **No Borrowing:** No borrowing or transfers are allowed between tiers.
