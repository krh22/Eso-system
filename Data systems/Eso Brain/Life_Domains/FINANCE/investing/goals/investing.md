*Version 1.0 · Nairobi, Kenya*

## 1. The Philosophy: Wealth Generation

The Investing Module is where Ryan moves from "Protecting Cash" to "Generating Yield". This module governs the allocation of business and investment funds to income-generating assets.

### Layer 1: Yield-Focused (The Anchor)
- **Goal:** Predictable monthly or quarterly income.
- **Assets:** Dividend stocks (NSE), MMF top-ups, or high-yield bonds.
- **Risk:** Low to Medium.

### Layer 2: Growth-Focused (The Build)
- **Goal:** Long-term capital appreciation.
- **Assets:** REITs (Real Estate Investment Trusts), Index Funds, or early-stage business equity.
- **Risk:** Medium to High.

---

## 2. Global Investing Rules (The Non-Negotiables)

1. **No Speculation:** Investing is not gambling. If the investment relies only on "number go up" without a clear underlying yield or utility, it is blocked.
2. **Diversification Cap:** No single asset or platform can hold more than 35% of the total Investment Portfolio.
3. **Re-investment Rule:** 50% of all dividends or interest earned must be automatically re-invested into the portfolio. The other 50% can be rolled into the Layer 2 Vault deployment.
4. **Platform Verification:** Only platforms with a proven track record (3+ years) or institutional backing are permitted for Layer 1 investments.
