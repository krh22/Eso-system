# FINANCE AI MANAGER — HOW TO USE

---

## 1. THE ONLY THING YOU DO DAILY

Every evening before bed, open a new Claude chat. Upload **FINANCE Daily log.md**. Then type what happened today in plain language. That is it. Claude does the rest.

**Example of what to type:**

> Spent 150 on food, 50 mat, 80 junk, girlfriend stayed so added 100 for snacks. Received 500 from mum. Balance now 800 mpesa 12k kcb.

**Claude will:**
1. Calculate your full day position.
2. Check every cap and rule.
3. Update all statistics.
4. Generate your morning to-do list for tomorrow.
5. Produce one Antigravity prompt to paste.

---

## 2. YOUR MORNING TO-DO LIST FORMAT

Claude generates this every evening. It appears at the top of your daily log. Format is always: numbered actions with exact amounts, one short paragraph of advice or observation, one flag if anything needs attention.

**Example output:**

> **Morning brief — 13 May 2026**
>
> 1. Your float today is KES 570 — company day. Do not exceed.
> 2. Food budget this week has KES 675 remaining. No extra food spending today.
> 3. Weed remaining this release KES 430. Next release day 15.
> 4. Mshwari balance KES 1,300. Do not withdraw today.
> 5. Mum KES 2,000 still pending. When it arrives do not spend it — add to KCB.
>
> **Observation:** You spent within rules yesterday. Two company days remaining this week. Your food stock from Monday covers most meals so keep junk spending low today.
>
> **Flag:** None today.

---

## 3. EMERGENCY REQUEST

Only if something urgent happens. Type:

> Emergency. What happened: [describe]. Amount needed: KES [amount].

Claude asks verification questions before approving anything.

---

## 4. BUYING REQUEST

Type:

> Want to buy [ITEM] for KES [AMOUNT].

Claude checks acquisition list and balance and approves or denies in one line.

---

## 5. STATISTICS AND PATTERNS

After 2 weeks of daily logs, Claude generates your first pattern report automatically on **Sunday 7 June 2026**. No action needed from you.

---

## 6. SAVING ACTIVATION

Saving does not start until your total balance stays above **KES 13,900** for two consecutive weeks. When that happens, Claude will flag it and ask if you are ready to activate. You say yes or no. No pressure. No automatic deductions until you confirm.

---

## 7. THE VISION

This finance system is one domain inside a personal **Life OS**. Future domains: food, health, exercise, time management, social. Each domain will connect to this one.

The morning to-do list will eventually include:
- Buy this food item because XYZ is running low.
- Take medicine.
- Go to gym.
- Call this person.

**One daily interaction. The system runs your life logistics. You run your life.**

---

**File management rule:** Never manually edit the daily log except to add your evening entry. Claude and Antigravity handle everything else.
